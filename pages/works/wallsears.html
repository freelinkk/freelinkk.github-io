the walls have ears, and they are listening
Composition for 4DSOUND system performed at The Concertgebouw
2024

When a sound is played in the halls of the Concertgebouw, it scatters and reflects; bringing an otherworldly resonance. In this process, sound decays, and a piece is inevitably lost and absorbed into the walls of the building. What do we hear when we listen as the walls do? Imagine this composition as a spatiotemporal inversion of the building; from the perspective of a small critter; scrambling their way through the walls, the pipes, electrical cabling & mechanical infrastructure.

To listen through the unheard ears of the building; an attempt to get in touch with the building's voice. A voice which acknowledges and shares the memories ingrained in its walls. To intentionally remove ourselves from a sonic aesthetic modernity that is imposed through the architecture and cultural historical context of the building.
