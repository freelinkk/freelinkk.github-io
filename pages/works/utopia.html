Utopia was/is
Interior Design - EP
11 March, 2022

Utopia was/is is the debut EP from Interior Design, an experimental music duo consisting of Marcus van Esveld and me. This EP is a culmination of the initial creative and artistic explorations performed and condensed into four tracks. These tracks were all started and completed in the attic, the name given to the inactive machine room of the former NVWA (Nederlandse Voedsel en Waren Autoriteit), which is now a social housing project.

The attic was a nearly yearlong temporary project, in which we created a studio and working space within the broken and dormant machinery of the building, as a way of experimenting with how sound and the music we created was intrinsically linked to the space, opening up ideas of what spaces are, and how we utilise, visualise, and hear them. A remnant of the intent in which the building was created, and now remains largely unused. This symbiotic discovery of this location as our working space meant we were constantly uncovering new aspects of the room. Recognising the space as being vital for ventilation and electricity: an intimate insight into the organs of the building. Advocating for the potentially undervalued knowledge that listening brings.

We heavily incorporated the existence of the attic into this EP and our music. Recording the material elements of this space; the drone of machinery, the clangs of metallic objects, the beeps of electricity, and heavily sampling many of these elements throughout the songs. Throughout the process of creation we also interrogated and focus on listening as a form of knowledge production, as a form of communication and reception to the machine room, to inform the process of creation.
