Sing the Body Electronic
Performance
Live at Our Place - Treehouse, NDSM
Nov 11, 2023

Running time: 39:15
Sing the Body Electronic is Amedeo Feingold and me
Photography & Filming by Andrew Kambel
Visuals by Niels Gercama

Sing the Body Electronic is a musical project consisting of Amedeo Feingold and me; born from the concept of searching for warmth in the ghost of electronic music. Recognising the potential that this combination of improvisation, samples, and acoustic instrumentation can bring to sonic immersion. This theme of warmth is what helps guide our approach to sonic storytelling; how we convey and connect distinct musical movements into a larger narrative. Weaving these movements together with self-recorded samples and field recordings; these personal auditory snapshots bring an emotional connection and connect to the themes we are concerned with: namely human connection (family, friendship), imminent ecological consequences of human living, and the feeling of the overwhelming city existence.

The performance was recorded from a mixer straight into a Zoom H4n and mastered by Sing the Body Electronic.

For the setup, we used a Korg Minilogue XD, a fender stratocaster, a piano, a Korg Kaoss Pad, a Pioneer DDJ-400, a Beatsep Pro, and two computers running instances of Ableton Live 11, all of which was being fed into a multichannel Soundcraft mixer.
